A Message To Humanity

📬 DEAR HUMANITY,

Welcome to the homepage for WHOMANATEE.

A 100% open knowledge, for all, not profit focused, family business for all HUMANITY.

A company focused on improving life for all humans on earth.

A company focused on making sure you never have to worry about paying basic living bills, like housing, food, education, etc.

A company focused on making our home-less home-full.

A company with zero employees, only owners. Everyone is a boss. Everyone is responsible.

Everyone gets to live the 🔥 NO BILL LIFE 🔥

All housing, food, healthcare, utilities, and more provided to all family business members by the family business.

Everyone is required to work for the family business, even the richest.

Everyone is required to learn for the family business, even the smartest.

Everyone is required to take vacation every year, even if you don't want to.

And everyone gets at least two months vacation per year, likely more.

Those that make money for the family business get increases in salary and family business spending budgets.

Those that support the family in some way that does not generate revenue, such as providing health services or other essentials, will be graciously provided for by the family business.

The more stressful and demanding the essential work, the more generous the providings.

Everyone at some point in their lifetime will get to take a sabbatical of at least 2 years continuous in order to work on personal ambitions.

The family business fully supports all family members in their endeavors, whatever they may be.

Family business resources are available for all family members to use on their personal projects.
