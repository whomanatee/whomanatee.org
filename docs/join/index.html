Everyone is required to work for the family business.

Everyone is required to learn for the family business.

Everyone is required to take vacation every year.

Everyone gets at least two months vacation per year.

Everyone at some point in their lifetime will get to take a sabbatical of at least 2 years continuous in order to work on personal ambitions.

The family business fully supports all family members in their endeavors, whatever they may be.

Family business resources are availalbe for all family members to use on their personal projects.

Those that make money for the family business get increases in salary and family business spending budgets.

Those that support the family in some way that does not generate revenue, such as providing health services or other essentials, will be taken care of by the family business.
